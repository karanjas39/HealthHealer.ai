About Departments

Primary Care Department consists of Primary care providers who are typically the first point of contact for patients seeking medical care for common health issues. They are trained to address a wide range of medical issues and provide general medical advice and treatment.

Cardiology Department provides information about the treatment of heart-related conditions. This includes conditions like heart disease, heart attack, and arrhythmia. Cardiologists may use a variety of tests, including ECGs and stress tests, to diagnose and treat these conditions.

Dermatology Department provides information about the treatment of skin-related conditions. This includes conditions like acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer. Dermatologists may use a variety of treatments, including medication and surgery, to manage these conditions.

Gastroenterology Department provides information about the treatment of digestive system disorders. This includes conditions like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), Crohn's disease, and ulcerative colitis. Gastroenterologists may use a variety of tests, including endoscopies and colonoscopies, to diagnose and treat these conditions.

Obstetrics and Gynecology Department provides information about the treatment of female reproductive system disorders. This includes conditions like infertility, menopause, and ovarian cysts. Obstetricians and gynecologists may use a variety of tests, including ultrasounds and Pap tests, to diagnose and treat these conditions.
